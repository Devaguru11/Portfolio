🐾 About Us

Pet-Friendly City is a community-driven initiative dedicated to transforming urban spaces into safe, inclusive, and joyful places for pets and their owners.

We organize events, build awareness, and work with local authorities to improve parks, streets, and facilities. Our team of passionate volunteers and partners are committed to making every city a better place for our furry friends.

Whether you're a pet parent, an animal lover, or a local changemaker, there's a place for you in our movement. Let's build a world where tails wag a little more every day!
